Intro to BumbleBeast

Oct 1, 2018 by Michael
Tags: general
So, What is BumbleBeast? It is a milling machine first - a monsterously powerful one at that. But it does plenty more than the standard desktop mill. Features It can machine aluminum alloys at optimal speeds and feeds, with accuracy of 25 microns (0.001 in.) or better. It has 5 axes of movement to reduce production time up to 90% for parts with multi-faced or organic features.
